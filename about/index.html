Our Story

Started With a Simple Question

Ben spent decades in the gym testing what disciplined effort can achieve — marathons, ultra-endurance events, CrossFit, HYROX and physique training. That taught him results rarely come from shortcuts; they come from consistency, careful preparation and doing the unglamorous work properly. After building a successful technology business, he created Pepilix as a passion project with one goal: a credible, transparent research-supply company you can actually trust.

We quickly learned that purity on paper means nothing if storage and cold-chain handling aren't taken just as seriously. So we built Pepilix around getting the unglamorous parts right — proper lyophilisation, verified HPLC purity on every batch, and cold-chain shipping — before anything else.

We're a small, family-run operation by choice. It means we personally check every order, answer our own messages, and stand behind everything that leaves our door.

Ben Davies

Founder & Operations Director
"Meaningful results don't come from shortcuts — they come from consistency and doing the work properly."

Ben has spent decades training in gyms and testing the limits of what disciplined effort can achieve — marathons, ultra-endurance events, CrossFit, HYROX training and, more recently, physique development. That journey taught him that results rarely come from shortcuts; they come from consistency, careful preparation and a willingness to keep working when a goal initially looks impossible.

After building a successful technology business, Ben created Pepilix as a passion project with a clear objective: a credible, transparent and accessible research-supply company backed by a genuine community.

He oversees Pepilix's operations, logistics, sourcing, storage protocols and compliance. Thousands of hours have gone into researching suppliers, reviewing documentation, refining storage processes and independently testing products — the standard expected of a professional research supplier.

Pepilix is not a faceless website. Ben remains directly involved and available to answer questions.
