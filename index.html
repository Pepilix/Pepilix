What is Pepilix and what products do you supply?▾
Pepilix is a UK-based supplier of research-grade lyophilised peptides and related laboratory compounds. All products are exclusively intended for controlled laboratory and research use by qualified professionals. Are Pepilix products suitable for human use?▾
No. All Pepilix products are strictly for laboratory research purposes only and are not approved, intended, or suitable for human or animal consumption, clinical use, or therapeutic applications.
Do you provide Certificates of Analysis?▾
Yes. Every Pepilix peptide order includes a Certificate of Analysis documenting peptide identity (verified via Mass Spectrometry), purity percentage (via HPLC), batch number, and date of manufacture.
How should I store my peptides?▾
Lyophilised peptides should be stored at -20°C for short-term use or -80°C for long-term storage. Once reconstituted, store at +2°C to +4°C and use within 20–30 days. Do not refreeze reconstituted peptides.

Storage Guidelines

Storage Advice

Correct storage is critical to maintaining peptide integrity and purity. Follow these guidelines to maximise shelf life.

❄️

Lyophilised Powder (Unopened)

Store sealed vials at −20°C for routine use or −80°C for long-term storage. Keep away from light and moisture. Properly stored lyophilised peptides remain stable for 24–36 months.

🧪

Reconstituted Solution

Once reconstituted, store at +2°C to +8°C. Use within 20–30 days. Never refreeze a reconstituted solution — this causes degradation and loss of potency.

💧

BAC Water

Store at +2°C to +25°C. Avoid freezing. Once opened, use within 28 days. Always use sterile technique when drawing from the vial.

🌡️

Temperature Excursions

Brief ambient temperature exposure during transit is generally tolerable for lyophilised peptides. Avoid prolonged heat above 25°C. Contact us if you have concerns about a specific compound.

🔒

Handling & Contamination

Always handle peptides in a clean environment using sterile syringes and needles. Wear appropriate PPE in accordance with your COSHH guidelines and institutional protocols.

⚠️

Research Use Only

All Pepilix compounds are for laboratory research purposes only. Not approved for human or veterinary use. Handle in accordance with all applicable safety regulations.

Laboratory Guidance

Laboratory Reconstitution & Handling Principles

These principles are provided for qualified laboratory personnel reconstituting lyophilised compounds within an institutional research setting. They are not instructions for individual or personal use, and do not replace your institution's own approved protocols and risk assessments.

1

Follow Your Institution's Approved Protocol

Reconstitution should only be carried out under your institution's own approved standard operating procedure, by personnel trained and authorised to do so. These general principles do not substitute for that protocol.

2

Aseptic Laboratory Technique

Reconstitution should be carried out using proper aseptic technique appropriate to a controlled laboratory environment — including a suitable clean work area, sterile consumables, and PPE in line with your institution's biosafety requirements.

3

Manufacturer & Laboratory Documentation

Refer to the Certificate of Analysis and any manufacturer documentation supplied with the compound before proceeding. Where documentation specifies particular handling conditions, those take precedence over general guidance.

4

Avoiding Contamination

Minimise agitation and avoid introducing air or particulates during solvent addition. Any solution showing cloudiness, particulates, or discolouration should not be used and should be logged and disposed of appropriately.

5

Recording Batch & Concentration

Reconstituted material should be labelled and logged in line with your institution's record-keeping requirements — including compound identity, batch number, concentration, and date — and traceable back to its Certificate of Analysis.

6

Validated Equipment & COSHH Disposal

Use only calibrated, validated laboratory equipment appropriate to the volumes involved. Waste and unused material should be disposed of in accordance with your institution's COSHH assessment and local chemical waste procedures.

These principles are general guidance only and do not constitute instructions for individual, personal, or non-laboratory use. Pepilix products are supplied strictly for use by qualified research professionals within a controlled institutional setting, in accordance with that institution's own protocols, risk assessments, and applicable regulations.
