FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pepilix and what products do you supply?▾
Pepilix is a UK-based supplier of research-grade lyophilised peptides and related laboratory compounds. All products are exclusively intended for controlled laboratory and research use by qualified professionals. We supply peptide powders, encapsulated compounds, and reconstitution solvents — all HPLC-tested and supplied with full Certificates of Analysis.
Are Pepilix products suitable for human use?▾
No. All Pepilix products are strictly for laboratory research purposes only and are not approved, intended, or suitable for human or animal consumption, clinical use, or therapeutic applications.
Do you provide Certificates of Analysis?▾
Yes. Every Pepilix peptide order includes a Certificate of Analysis documenting peptide identity (verified via Mass Spectrometry), purity percentage (via HPLC), batch number, and date of manufacture.
How should I store my peptides?▾
Lyophilised peptides should be stored at -20°C for short-term use or -80°C for long-term storage. Once reconstituted, store at +2°C to +4°C and use within 20–30 days. Do not refreeze reconstituted peptides.
What are your delivery options?▾
UK orders placed before 2PM (Mon–Fri) or 12PM (Sat) are dispatched same-day via tracked next-day delivery. International orders ship via tracked courier with an estimated 3–7 working day delivery.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept bank transfer, Bitcoin, Ethereum, and PayPal Friends & Family (via WhatsApp). Free next-day UK delivery on orders over £150 — orders under £150 include a £6.99 delivery charge.

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us at hello@pepilix.com within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and photographic evidence. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

2. Non-Delivery

If your order has not arrived within the expected timeframe, contact us and we will investigate with the courier. If confirmed lost in transit, we will reship or issue a full refund.

3. Change of Mind

We are unable to accept returns or issue refunds for change-of-mind purchases on research compounds once dispatched, due to cold-chain integrity requirements and regulatory obligations.

4. Order Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled and fully refunded if cancellation is requested before dispatch. Contact us immediately at hello@pepilix.com or by phone. Once dispatched, cancellation is not possible.

5. Bank Transfer Orders

For orders paid by bank transfer that are subsequently cancelled prior to dispatch, refunds will be processed within 3–5 business days back to the originating account.

6. Refund Timescales

Approved refunds are processed within 5 business days. Bank transfer refunds may take an additional 1–3 days to clear depending on your bank.

Shipping Policy

Delivery timescales, packaging, and international shipping information.

Last updated: April 2026 · Pepilix Research Ltd, Registered in England & Wales

1. UK Dispatch & Delivery

Orders placed before 2:00PM Monday–Friday or 12:00PM Saturday are dispatched same day via tracked next-day courier. Orders placed after the cutoff or on Sunday will be dispatched the following business day. Free next-day delivery is included on all orders over £150. Orders under £150 incur a £6.99 delivery charge.

2. Packaging

All peptide compounds are shipped in discreet, temperature-controlled packaging. Lyophilised peptides are packaged with appropriate cold-chain insulation to maintain integrity during transit. Packaging is plain with no external branding indicating contents.

3. International Shipping

We ship internationally via tracked courier services. Estimated delivery times:

  • Europe: 2–4 working days
  • USA & Canada: 4–7 working days
  • Australia & Asia Pacific: 5–10 working days
  • Rest of World: 5–14 working days

International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and order weight.

4. Import Duties & Customs

International customers are responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees applicable in their country. Pepilix Research Ltd cannot be held liable for delays caused by customs processing.

5. Regulatory Compliance

It is the customer's responsibility to ensure that importing research compounds complies with applicable laws and regulations in their country. We cannot ship to countries where such products are prohibited. Orders to restricted destinations will be cancelled and refunded.

6. Tracking

A tracking number will be sent to your email address once your order has been dispatched. Use this to monitor your delivery via the courier's website.

7. Failed Delivery

If a delivery attempt is unsuccessful, the courier will leave a card and attempt redelivery or hold at a local depot. Unclaimed parcels returned to us will be reshipped upon request — a redelivery charge may apply.
