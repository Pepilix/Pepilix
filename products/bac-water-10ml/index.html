Frequently Asked Questions

What is BAC Water used for?

Bacteriostatic water is used to reconstitute lyophilised peptide powders into solution for laboratory research use. It contains 0.9% benzyl alcohol, which helps prevent bacterial growth across multiple draws from the same vial.

Is BAC Water suitable for human use?

No. All Pepilix products, including BAC Water, are strictly for laboratory research purposes only and are not approved, intended, or suitable for human or animal consumption, clinical use, or therapeutic applications.

How long can I use an opened vial of BAC Water?
